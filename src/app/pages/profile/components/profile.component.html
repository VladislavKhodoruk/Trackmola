Instructions for creating a report

Reports give you the ability to present your work activity to managers.
It is better for you to implement your activity daily so that your manager can generate a weekly report without you at the end of the week.
You can also create weekly report in TrackMola individually, just clicking a button "send report for approval" in the My report.
